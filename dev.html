FAQs

What’s the dress code?

Come as you are! We’re there for a good time, not a long time, so we want you to feel confident in your own personal style. We also don’t expect you to purchase new attire that won’t be worn again. Remember, it’s still a wedding, so ideally somewhere between smart casual and cocktail attire will nail it. Our venue / theme is leaning towards vintage and rock’n’roll, if you need some inspiration.

Where should we book accommodation?

We suggest anywhere between Broadbeach and Burleigh Heads, to best enjoy what the Gold Coast has to offer and still be within a close drive to the venue.

What’s the parking and transport situation?

No parking onsite. There is street parking available. Uber and taxis are readily available in the area.

Are children allowed?

With respect, this is an adults-only affair. Unless you wish to offer them as a sacrifice.

Is this a satanic wedding?

We can understand why you’d think so! It’s just a relaxed, light-hearted ceremony with a sprinkle of dark humour, followed by (hopefully) one hell of a party!

What’s the go during the reception?

Seating will be available, but will not be allocated. A variety of canapes will be served while you are free to mingle!

What’s the bar situation?

Bar service will commence at 4pm, so that you can enjoy the ceremony with a cold one in-hand! There will also be a range of non-alcoholic options available for those who wish to prioritise their livers.

We can’t wait to celebrate with you!
